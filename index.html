About

I only publish my works under the pseudonym Grey Tekin.
This virtual character is sometimes a main actor, a supporting actor or just a viewer of the resulting digital artworks. He currently perceives the world in black, white and gray. Therefore, his artworks are mainly finalized in shades of gray. All works are created from a mix of different multimedia tools like:
